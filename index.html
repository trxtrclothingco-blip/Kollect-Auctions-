About Kollect Auctions

Kollect Auctions was started by Peter James, a lifetime collector and fan of all things collectible. We created Kollect to share these rare and exciting items with the world. Our platform connects collectors with auctions for comics, cards, antiques, and other unique treasures, providing a secure and engaging experience for every enthusiast.
